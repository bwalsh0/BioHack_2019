What is BioHack?

Founded in 2019 by student leaders, BioHack is a 12-hour bio-engineering related hackathon at UCR. Our mission is to foster connections among students and sponsors throughout the computer science and bio-engineering industries. Through BioHack students will form teams to work on projects, attend techincal workshops, speak with mentors, and network with industries.
