WHAT

Founded in 2019 by student leaders, BioHack is a 12-hour bioengineering related hackathon at UCR.

IS

Through BioHack, students will form teams to work on projects, attend technical workshop, and network.

BIOHACK?

BioHack is an event where students can create medically innovative projects and technologies.
